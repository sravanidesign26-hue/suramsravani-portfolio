A little about me

I'm a 2025 B.Tech CSE graduate with a UI/UX certification, based in Hyderabad. I got into design because I wanted to build things people who don't consider themselves "tech-savvy" could still use confidently — which is why so much of my work lives in fintech and everyday utility apps.

Alongside client and portfolio projects, I'm building a presence around UI/UX design content for Tier 2 and Tier 3 audiences in India — a perspective I think is underrepresented in design content today.

I also design and build — going from a Figma file to a working front end in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
